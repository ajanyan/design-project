 About RACIS 2018

Welcome to the official website of the National Conference on Recent Advancements in Computing and Information Science 2018 (RACIS ’18) scheduled to be held at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, NSS College of Engineering, Palakkad, Kerala on 19th and 20th of January 2018.

Computing in the most simplest of terms is the method of using computers to accomplish a task. The field of computing includes computer engineering, software engineering, computer science, information systems, and information technology. The field of information science brings together and uses the theories, principles, techniques and technologies of a variety of disciplines toward the solution of information problems. The objective of this national conference is to provide a forum for researchers, academicians, industry practitioners and students to interact, exchange and share their experiences, ideas and research results about all aspects in the field of computing and information science as well as to identify emerging research topics and define future directions in these fields.

 About Computer Science & Engineering Department

The department of computer science & engineering is the latest addition to the Institution in the academic year 1999 - 2000. Identification of India as a potential source of computer professionals and keeping in mind the employment potential in this sector, the department aims at providing competent professionals to contribute its share to this technology boom. Faculty members and supporting staff with rich experience are appointed in the department.

 About NSSCE

N S S College of Engineering, Palakkad is one of the most reputed, premier engineering educational Institution in Kerala. Founded in 1960 by Nair Service Society under the leadership of late Bharatha Kesari Mannathu Padmanabhan, during the second five year plan with the assistance of the Central and State Government under the grant-in-aid scheme, affiliated to the Kerala Technological University, the College has an enviable heritage and legacy of grooming brilliant engineering professionals who later made their mark in industrial and other sectors of the country and abroad.

Situated in NSS Nagar at Akathethara, 9 km from Palakkad town, the Campus is easily accessible, being only 4 km from the Palakkad Railway Junction, and is only 5 km from the renowned Malampuzha Gardens. Nearest Airports are at Coimbatore (55Kkm) and Cochin International (110 Km). Spread over 125 Acres, it includes an Administrative Block, Separate blocks for each Department, A Library block and four Hostels including one for Ladies. Nestling among verdant foothills, this is a tranquil and serene setting for higher education.

 Paper Submission

Length of the paper should be limited to a maximum of 6 pages in IEEE conference double column format.

All papers will be peer reviewed and the review comments will be communicated to the authors. At least one author should register for the conference.All accepted and registered papers will be published in the conference proceedings and soft copy of the same shall be made available to the participants.

Some excellent works will be invited to be extended and re-submitted to undergo the review for forwarding to some reputed journals (SCI/SCIE/SCOPUS indexed) for further review and publication.

 Keynote Speakers

Dr. Anitha Professor & Head in the Department of CSE, Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering, Sriperumbudur

With an experience of 16 years in Teaching & Research, Dr. R. Anitha is currently working at Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering, Sriperumbudur as Professor & Head in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering. Dr. Anitha received her UG from Bharathidasan University and her PG and Ph.D from Anna University, Chennai-25. She was a meritorious candidate during her entire course of studies. She was a Silver Medalists in her UG and a Gold Medalists in her PG studies. During her Ph.D, she has received Senior Research Fellowship under meritorious scheme from UGC-New Delhi. Her primary research interests are in the field of Grid & Cloud Computing, Big Data Analytics and Distributed systems. Specifically, she is interested in Cloud Security & Data storage in large scale distributed databases. As recognition towards her research work Dr. Anitha has received Grants from CSIR-New Delhi to present her research work in University of Washington, Seattle, USA in the year 2013. She has published over 29 research papers in various refereed international journals and in the proceedings of international conferences. Anitha’s professional interests focus on guiding many fellows towards their Ph.D. At present she is guiding seven scholars under Anna University. She acts a nodal officer for Vishweshvaraya Fellowship at Zone 2. As a principal investigator, she is currently involved in one funded project of worth Rs. 24 lakhs from DST-SERB, New Delhi Project titled "DigiCert: Data Security in Federated DigiCloud Environment using Homomorphic Technique". Two of her project proposals are under review at DRDO & Department of Science & Technology (DST).

Mr. Arjun P R Senior Research Engineer,
Centre for Development of Telematics
Govt. of India

Arjun P R is a Senior Research Engineer at Centre for Development of Telematics, Govt. Of India, which is an organization creating solutions for current and future requirements of telecommunication and converged networks of national importance in rural and strategic sector. An alumni of computer science department of NSS College of Engineering Palakkad, he obtained his Post Graduation from NIT Calicut in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. His academic interests include Machine Vision and Image Processing. With 6 years of experience, Arjun works as designer and developer in Call Processing module of LTE-A Radio Access System and Rural GSM system aimed at providing low cost and rigid telecommunication system for India. He also developed an automation framework for the organization to manage complete life cycle of a product thereby reducing manual effort and contributing towards process improvements. He also has a passion for Android Application development.
